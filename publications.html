PUBLICATIONS

The following publications contain information on mine site simulation and visualisation projects. The research papers were written by Dr. P Stothard and colleagues. The information is placed here for research purposes and is intended as resource for those interested in applying virtual reality to industry. All colleagues, contributors and project sponsors are acknowledged.
