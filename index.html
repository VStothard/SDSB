INDUSTRIAL VISUALISATION & SIMULATION

Simulation Design Services is a specialist 3D Visualisation company founded in 1999 and based in Sydney, NSW.

We provide visualisation solutions to clients within the metalliferous mining, coal mining, oil and gas, construction, building, engineering, geoscience and power industries within Australia.

Simulation Design Services is a small, highly creative and collaborative team of specialists who are passionate about 3D visualisation and simulation development, and are driven to provide sustainable visualisations to industry.

Our visualisation solutions include the provision of services that can provide 360 video, 360 panoramic images, virtual reality simulation and augmented reality simulation. We identify appropriate solutions for industry via an industry needs analysis.

Industry and Businesses Applications

Computer based visualisations have proven extremely valuable in cases where the real work environment is difficult to access or creates issues with logistics and safety if people have to go on site to inspect issues. Also, if the site does not already exist, visualisations are useful for assessing new environments or proposed changes to an existing one. This is especially the case in industries such as mining, oil and gas or construction where training in real life situations or events is imperative to the success of safe and sustainable organisational operations, but where there is a high risk of injury or death if mistakes are made in a real life training situation.

Interactive visualisations and simulations of industrial environments can be used to develop safe work procedures, safety management plans and risk assessments via team collaboration, and the presentation of complex data in an intuitive graphical format that is easily comprehended by experts and non-experts alike. Mistakes can be made in a safe and forgiving environment, ensuring the safety of all employees while introducing them to the risk and hazards that may be associated with their work environment. Business assets remain in service and people are better prepared for routine and emergency operations.
