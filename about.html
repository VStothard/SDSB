ABOUT SDS

Simulation Design Services provide consulting and technical services for the integration of advanced visualisation, simulation, virtual reality and augmented reality technologies into industry.

Our focus areas are robust and effective system design, human computer interfaces, environment visualisation, spatial awareness training simulators and hazardous site environment visualisations and data visualisation. All solutions are tailored to the needs of the client and systems can be used for training and inductions, geological data visualisation, remote operations visualisation, site simulations, industrial equipment deployment simulations, OH&S compliance or systems control.

Simulation Design Services develop solutions for metalliferous mining, coal mining, oil and gas, industrial and commercial construction, residential building, engineering, geology, geoscience and power industries within Australia.

Simulation Design Services is based in the South West of Sydney NSW.
